Using QueepBot

    QueepBot diagnoses standardization errors in OPR/EPR bullets. To use, paste your evaluation bullets in the textbox below. Inconsistently used acronyms are highlighted in red, while approved acronyms are highlighted in blue. Hover your mouse over the highlighted word to view more information. To see an example, type: "ldr" and "leader" into the input box. This will be flagged as inconsistent, because ldr and leader are not standardized.

    Other features include identificaiton of common adminstrative errors. For example: A "!" needs to be placed at the end of a bullet, or have two spaces following it. A "--" must not have whitespace on either side. Support for customized acronym lists and administrative errors will be released in the future.
